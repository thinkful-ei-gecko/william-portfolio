About Me

Hi, I'm William. I'm an aspiring full-stack developer who loves to code!

I began my professional career as a financial consultant. Yet, something in my career seemed to be lacking. I’ve always enjoyed exercising my creativity, but that wasn’t a skill regularly used in the finance sector.

Cue, coding! I see it as the perfect marriage of creativity and logic. It’s fascinating how you can turn ideas into tangible products with an analytical mind. I felt that with my finance background, it would help ease the transition into coding since I could apply my problem-solving and teaming skills I’ve developed in my former profession. To me, coding provides a strong sense of fulfillment and excitement. It’s a wonderful feeling when finally getting a piece of your code to work!

Outside of coding, you can often find me playing games (MMOs, RPGs, or a combination of both), playing pick-up basketball, or finding new, delicious restaurants to enjoy with my friends.
